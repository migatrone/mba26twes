Mba26Twes is a Moroccan music producer and artist who has quickly risen as a prominent figure in the modern trap and hip-hop scene. Hailing from Marrakech, he grew up immersed in a vibrant culture of sounds and rhythms, which inspired his unique musical approach. Combining hard-hitting beats, melodic synths, and experimental soundscapes, Mba26Twes crafts tracks that not only move bodies but also evoke deep emotions.

Influenced by global icons like Travis Scott, Playboi Carti, Kendrick Lamar, and Metro Boomin, Mba26Twes has developed a distinctive style that merges the intensity of street energy with artistic sophistication. From producing in FL Studio to experimenting with vocal manipulation, every track reflects his dedication to innovation and storytelling through music.

Beyond the studio, Mba26Twes is known for his creativity, curiosity, and drive to push boundaries. His work resonates with listeners worldwide, bridging cultural gaps and creating a sound that is unmistakably his own. As he continues to release music, Mba26Twes is shaping the future of Moroccan hip-hop, making a mark on the international stage with his electrifying productions and visionary artistry.
